APTs e Cybercrime

os dois sao considerados criminais mas geralmente tem diferentes motivacoes e objetivos

APT - intwligencia estrategica, espionagem, objetivos politos
Cybercrime - financial gain, data resale, extortion

Tempo operacional
APT - paciente, intrusoes de longo prazo, stealth & persistence
Cybercrime - fast, alto volume de operacoes, automacoes e escala - quanto mais possam automatizar o cybercrime melhor

ferramentas
APT - custom implants (sometimes commodoty tools, modular toolchains, custom loaders
Cybercrime commodity RATs, info-stealers, builders, crypters

Infraestrutura
APT- resiliente, multi-hop C2, opsec (often compromised infracstructure)
Cybercrime - cloud hosting, bulletproof hosts, short-lived domains

Typical targets
APT- governo, infraestrutura critica, defesa, instituicoes de pesquisa
Cybercrime - enterprise, SMBs, online consumers, payment platforms

Exemplos 
APT - style behavior: targeted spear-pishing, multi stage implant, long-live C2
Cybercime - mass phishing, credential-harvesting kits, IABs, exploitation of vulns to sell info
